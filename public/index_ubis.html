INTEGRATION SESSION

We prepare a bank of 2-hour integration sessions/meetings. We use them as a building blocks, for a workshop agenda. All existing integration procedures (channels and interfaces setup, SOA services triggering, data mapping etc.) are presented in the bank. Each session splits into two steps: half hour functional overview and hour and a half questioner go thru. A questioneer hold basic behaviour as OOB configuration. The outcome of the questioneer is a integration setup document (basic rules and profile attributes), which could be used lateley as a reference setup. We also provide a two weeks support in order to finilize proposed setup(extention is optional) and bank of 24 hours production support.
