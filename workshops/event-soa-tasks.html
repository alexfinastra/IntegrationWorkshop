GPP is a multi-office system where all offices share the same application server but also equire performing these maintenance activities according to the office local time. In other ord, the maintenance activities are done at the office local time and for the office data only. At the end of each business day, GPP must engage in sequential Start of day (SOD) activities in preparation for the following business day. Note: Banks can set up scripts for SOD activities epending on their operational and functional needs:  Run SOD at the end of the previous day  un SOD at the beginning of a business day The time of day may also vary, for example performing OD at 18:00 and starting to work as if the next day has begun while in fact the relevant essages will only be sent the following day. GPP supports the following:  If needed, database ackup can be done while the system is online, which means that users can continue working on it.  Running SOD per office (this means that offices in different countries run SOD according to their local time).
